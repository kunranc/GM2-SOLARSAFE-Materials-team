# Microscope Surface Imaging Protocol

## Purpose
Document surface condition of plastic samples before and after NaOCl treatment using optical microscopy.

## Equipment
- Optical microscope
- Camera/imaging attachment
- Plastic samples (PP, PVC, Nylon) after NaOCl treatment

## Procedure
1. Mount sample flat on microscope stage
2. Select appropriate magnification (suggested: 40x and 100x)
3. Focus on sample surface
4. Capture images of at least 3 representative areas per sample
5. Compare treated vs control samples

## What to look for
- Surface cracks or crazing
- Discolouration
- Pitting or erosion
- Changes in surface texture or roughness

## Notes
- Profilometry was unavailable for this study; microscopy provides qualitative surface assessment only
- Save images as PNG with filename: [material]_[condition]_[magnification].png
