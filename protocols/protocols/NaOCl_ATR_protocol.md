# NaOCl ATR-FTIR Protocol

## Purpose
Assess chemical degradation of plastic bag materials after NaOCl exposure.

## Materials
- PVC, PP, and Nylon samples (2x2 cm)
- 3% NaOCl solution
- 5% NaOCl solution
- Distilled water (control)
- ATR-FTIR spectrometer
- Optical microscope

## Conditions tested
1. Water (control)
2. 3% NaOCl
3. 5% NaOCl
4. 5% NaOCl + heat (40 degrees C)

## Procedure
1. Cut plastic samples to uniform size
2. Submerge in respective solutions for the test duration
3. Remove, rinse with distilled water, dry at room temperature
4. Acquire ATR-FTIR spectrum for each sample
5. Compare spectra: new peaks at 3300 and 1715 cm-1 indicate degradation
6. Image surface under optical microscope

## Analysis
- Red zone (3300, 1715 cm-1): new peaks = oxidation
- Green zone (fingerprint): disappearing peaks = backbone breakdown
- Document surface changes under microscope
